## Brand Colors

The logo uses Duro's signature colors:
- **Primary Red**: `#EB5757` - Used for geometric accent shapes
- **Text Black**: `#333333` - Used for the DURO wordmark and border

## Usage Guidelines

- Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background
- Minimum width: 136px for legibility
- Provide clear space equal to the logo's height on all sides
